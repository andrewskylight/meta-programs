Meta-Tracker

Keep track of all meta-programs

With an overwhelming number of meta-programs available it is quite easy to get lost.

Fortunately, this app allows you to keep track of all meta-programs easily and conveniently.

Meta-programs on a range continium.

Most patterns exist on a continium. Values in between represent a sequence between extreme values of meta-programs.

Meta-programs with different patterns

Separate components had been developed for patterns that do not fall into continium category.
